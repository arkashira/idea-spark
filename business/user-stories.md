# User Stories

## Epic 1: Idea Generation

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to input my interests and skills,
**So that** the tool can generate tailored product ideas.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool accepts text input for interests and skills.
  - The tool generates at least 10 product ideas based on the input.
  - The ideas are relevant to the inputted interests and skills.
- Complexity: M

**As a** solo creator,
**I want** to see trending topics in the creator economy,
**So that** I can align my product ideas with market demand.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool displays trending topics in the creator economy.
  - The topics are updated regularly (at least weekly).
  - The topics are relevant to the creator economy.
- Complexity: S

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to filter ideas by potential market size,
**So that** I can focus on ideas with higher revenue potential.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool allows filtering ideas by estimated market size.
  - The market size estimates are based on reliable data sources.
  - The filtering is intuitive and easy to use.
- Complexity: L

## Epic 2: Idea Validation

**As a** solo creator,
**I want** to see competitor analysis for each idea,
**So that** I can assess the competitive landscape.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool provides competitor analysis for each generated idea.
  - The analysis includes direct and indirect competitors.
  - The analysis is presented in an easy-to-understand format.
- Complexity: L

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to get an estimate of the development effort required,
**So that** I can prioritize ideas based on feasibility.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool provides an estimate of development effort for each idea.
  - The estimate considers technical complexity and required resources.
  - The estimate is presented in a clear and concise manner.
- Complexity: M

**As a** solo creator,
**I want** to see potential monetization strategies,
**So that** I can plan how to generate revenue from the idea.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool suggests potential monetization strategies for each idea.
  - The strategies are tailored to the specific idea.
  - The strategies are presented in a clear and actionable format.
- Complexity: M

## Epic 3: User Experience

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to save and organize my favorite ideas,
**So that** I can easily revisit and develop them later.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool allows users to save ideas to a personal library.
  - Users can organize ideas into categories or projects.
  - The saved ideas are easily accessible and searchable.
- Complexity: S

**As a** solo creator,
**I want** to receive regular updates on new ideas and trends,
**So that** I can stay informed and inspired.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool sends regular updates on new ideas and trends.
  - Users can customize the frequency and content of updates.
  - The updates are delivered via email or in-app notifications.
- Complexity: S

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to collaborate with other users on idea development,
**So that** I can leverage collective knowledge and skills.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool allows users to share ideas and collaborate with others.
  - Users can comment on and contribute to each other's ideas.
  - The collaboration features are integrated seamlessly into the tool.
- Complexity: L

## Epic 4: Performance and Integration

**As a** solo creator,
**I want** the tool to generate ideas quickly,
**So that** I can iterate and validate ideas efficiently.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool generates ideas in under 5 seconds.
  - The tool maintains high performance even with large datasets.
  - The tool provides feedback during the generation process.
- Complexity: M

**As a** indie hacker,
**I want** to integrate the tool with my existing workflow,
**So that** I can use it seamlessly alongside other tools.

- Acceptance Criteria:
  - The tool offers integration with popular productivity and project management tools.
  - The integration is easy to set up and use.
  - The tool maintains data consistency across integrated platforms.
- Complexity: L
