```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Indie hacker or solo creator feels stuck or uninspired, seeking new product ideas.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming amount of information, lack of direction, difficulty in identifying high-value ideas.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, anxiety, uncertainty.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide curated, high-quality idea prompts and success stories.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the product's landing page.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User becomes aware of Idea-Spark through social media, forums, or word-of-mouth.
- **Friction Points**: Skepticism about the tool's effectiveness, concerns about cost or time investment.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious, seeking validation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials, testimonials, and case studies showcasing successful idea validation.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from landing page to sign-up.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free trial or demo of Idea-Spark.
- **Friction Points**: Learning curve, initial setup, understanding how to use the tool effectively.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension, eager to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide onboarding tutorials, quick-start guides, and responsive customer support.
- **Metric**: Number of active users during the trial period.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User experiences initial success with idea generation and validation.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing workflows, cost considerations, scalability.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief, growing confidence in the tool.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized onboarding, regular updates, and community support.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User achieves significant success with Idea-Spark and seeks to maximize its potential.
- **Friction Points**: Need for advanced features, scalability, and team collaboration tools.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm, ambition, desire for growth.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Introduce advanced features, team collaboration options, and exclusive content.
- **Metric**: Number of upsells or upgrades to premium plans.
```
