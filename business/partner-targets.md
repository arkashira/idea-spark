# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Idea-Spark

### Target Integrations

1. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to automate workflows by connecting Idea-Spark with thousands of other apps, enhancing productivity for indie hackers.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Automates idea validation processes by connecting with tools like Google Sheets, Slack, and Trello.

2. **Notion**
   - **Rationale**: Allows users to organize and manage their ideas within a versatile workspace, enhancing idea tracking and development.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited pages and blocks, but limited to 1,000 blocks for databases.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates idea documentation and collaboration, aiding in the refinement of product concepts.

3. **Airtable**
   - **Rationale**: Provides a flexible database solution for users to manage their idea validation processes effectively.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 1,200 records per base, 2 GB attachment space.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users track and analyze their product ideas and feedback in a structured format.

4. **Product Hunt**
   - **Rationale**: A platform for launching and discovering new products, allowing users to validate their ideas with a community of early adopters.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Free to post products, but limited visibility without paid promotions.
   - **Integration Effort**: Low
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates exposure and feedback for new ideas, aiding in market validation.

5. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating payment processing allows users to monetize their ideas quickly and efficiently.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: No monthly fees, but transaction fees apply (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction).
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies the process of setting up payments for validated product ideas, enhancing revenue generation.

6. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Enhances team collaboration and communication around idea generation and validation processes.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 10,000 message history, 1:1 video calls.
   - **Integration Effort**: Low
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates real-time discussions and feedback on product ideas, improving collaboration among creators.

7. **Figma**
   - **Rationale**: A design tool that allows users to create prototypes and mockups for their product ideas.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 3 projects, unlimited files in drafts.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Enables users to visualize and iterate on their ideas, enhancing the development process.

8. **Google Analytics**
   - **Rationale**: Provides insights into user behavior and engagement with product ideas, aiding in data-driven decision-making.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Up to 10 million hits per month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users validate their ideas through analytics, ensuring they meet market demands.

### Conclusion
Integrating with these platforms will enhance the functionality of Idea-Spark, providing indie hackers and solo creators with the tools they need to generate, validate, and monetize their product ideas effectively. Prioritizing partnerships with affiliate/revenue-share opportunities will also create additional revenue streams for Axentx.
