### 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)  

| Persona | Daily Job / Pain | Monthly Budget for Tools | Why Idea‑Spark Wins |
|---------|------------------|--------------------------|----------------------|
| **Mia “Micro‑SaaS” Patel** – Founder of a 1‑person SaaS startup | Spends 2‑3 hrs daily scanning Reddit, Product Hunt, and newsletters for the next niche SaaS; suffers from analysis paralysis and low conversion from ideas to MVP. | **$50‑$100** (analytics, low‑code, marketing tools) | Needs a rapid, data‑backed idea filter to cut research time by >70 % and focus on execution. |
| **Liam “Live‑Creator” Gomez** – Twitch streamer & merch seller | Manages live streams, community engagement, and occasional merch drops; wants new revenue streams but lacks product‑ideation bandwidth. | **$30‑$70** (design assets, merch platforms) | Wants ready‑made, creator‑centric product concepts (e.g., digital goods, community tools) that align with his audience. |
| **Sofia “Side‑Hustle” Nguyen** – Full‑time marketer building side‑projects | Works 9‑5 marketing job, devotes evenings to side‑hustles; struggles to validate demand before spending development hours. | **$20‑$80** (email tools, landing‑page builders) | Values a quick “idea‑to‑validation” loop to decide which side‑project to pursue first. |

*Total addressable ICP size (US & EU): ~250 k solo creators, ~45 % actively paying for productivity SaaS → **~112 k potential paying users**.*

| Channel | Tactics (first 90 days) | CAC (USD) | Rationale |
|---------|------------------------|-----------|-----------|
| **1️⃣ Paid Community Sponsorships** (e.g., Indie Hackers, Product Hunt Discord, Makerpad) | • Sponsored AMA + free‑trial coupon <br>• Targeted retargeting ads to members who engaged | **$45** | High intent audience; cost per lead ≈ $12, conversion to paid ≈ 35 % → CAC ≈ $45. |
| **2️⃣ Content‑Driven SEO + Guest Posts** | • Publish “Idea‑Spark Playbooks” on Medium & personal blogs <br>• Guest post on *NoCode.Tech*, *Side Hustle Stack* | **$30** | Organic traffic acquisition cost amortized over 6 months; early adopters convert at 20 % after 2‑3 visits. |
| **3️⃣ Referral / Affiliate Program** | • 20 % revenue share for each referred paying user (first 6 months) <br>• Provide pre‑made embed widgets for bloggers | **$20** | Low upfront spend; word‑of‑mouth in creator circles drives high‑quality leads. |

*Target blended CAC for the first 90 days: **≈ $35**.*
